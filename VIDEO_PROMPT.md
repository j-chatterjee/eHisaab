## 🎬 Full Tutorial Video Prompt

```
Create a 3-minute tutorial video for a mobile app called "eHisaab" — a free, offline expense-splitting Progressive Web App (PWA).

Visual style: Clean screen-recording style with finger-tap highlights on mobile. Use soft purple (#6c63ff) as accent color. Add smooth slide-in transitions between scenes. Display step titles as animated text overlays at the bottom of each scene.

Voiceover tone: Friendly, clear, Indian-English accent preferred. Upbeat but professional.

---

INTRO (0:00 – 0:15)
Show the eHisaab app icon (💸) on a phone home screen. Tap to open.
Voiceover: "Tired of awkward money conversations after trips and parties? Meet eHisaab — the free app that tracks who paid what, and tells you exactly who owes whom. No login to a server, no subscriptions. Your data stays on your phone."

---

SCENE 1 – Sign Up (0:15 – 0:30)
Show the sign-up screen. Type a name, email, and password. Tap "Sign Up".
Voiceover: "Getting started is easy. Just sign up with your name and email — and you're in. Next time, just sign in."

---

SCENE 2 – Import Contacts (0:30 – 0:45)
Tap the avatar icon (top right). Tap "My Contacts". Tap "Import from Phone". Show contacts appearing in the list.
Voiceover: "Before creating a group, import your phone contacts. This lets you pick members with just a tap — no typing needed."

---

SCENE 3 – Create a Group (0:45 – 1:05)
Tap "+ New Group". Type "Goa Trip 2026". Select currency ₹. Choose "✈️ Trip". Show the contacts checkbox list — your name is at the top marked "You". Check 3 friends. Tap "Create Group".
Voiceover: "Tap New Group, give it a name, pick your currency and category. Your contacts appear with checkboxes — you're automatically added. Select your friends and create the group."

---

SCENE 4 – Add Members Manually (1:05 – 1:15)
Inside the group, go to Members tab. Type a name "Rahul". Add a WhatsApp number. Tap Add.
Voiceover: "You can also add members manually and save their WhatsApp number for payment reminders."

---

SCENE 5 – Add an Expense (1:15 – 1:40)
Tap "+ Add Expense". Type "Hotel Stay". Enter ₹3000. Show the "Paid by" dropdown — it already says "Me (Priya)" highlighted. Leave it as-is. Select "Equal" split. Tap Save. Show the expense appear in the list.
Voiceover: "Adding an expense is simple — describe it, enter the amount, and pick how to split it. The Paid by field defaults to Me — so if you paid, just leave it. Change it only if someone else paid."

---

SCENE 6 – Instant Balance Summary (1:40 – 2:00)
Scroll up to show the 4 stat chips. Below them highlight the colour-coded debt row: red chip "You → Rahul: ₹750", green chip "Priya → You: ₹400". Then tap the Balances tab to show the full member table.
Voiceover: "The moment you add an expense, the stats bar updates instantly. Red means you owe, green means someone owes you. Open the Balances tab for a full breakdown — every member's total paid and net balance in one view."

---

SCENE 7 – Settle & WhatsApp Reminder (2:00 – 2:15)
Tap the WhatsApp button next to Rahul → show a pre-filled WhatsApp message opening. Then tap "Settle" → confirm → balance updates to Settled ✓.
Voiceover: "Send a WhatsApp reminder in one tap. Once they pay, mark it as Settled — the balance updates instantly."

---

SCENE 8 – Party / Bottle Calculator (2:15 – 2:30)
Create a group with category 🎉 Party. In Members tab show the Bottle Calculator — enter Beer, bottle size 650ml, price ₹180, counts per person. Show per-person drink cost auto-calculated.
Voiceover: "Planning a party with drinks? Use the Bottle Calculator — enter what was bought, the price, and how many glasses each person had. eHisaab calculates each person's drink cost automatically."

---

SCENE 9 – Settings & Security (2:30 – 2:45)
Tap avatar → "Set PIN Lock" → enter 4-digit PIN. Show the PIN screen appearing on next open. Tap avatar → "Export Backup" → show JSON file saved.
Voiceover: "Protect your data with a PIN lock — the app won't open without it. And always export a backup so you never lose your expense history."

---

OUTRO (2:45 – 3:00)
Show app on phone and tablet side by side. Display URL or QR code.
Voiceover: "eHisaab — free, offline, and always on your side. Open it in any browser, add it to your home screen, and start splitting fairly today."

---

End card: App name "eHisaab 💸", tagline "Split bills. Not friendships.", GitHub Pages URL
```

## 📋 Screen Recording Tips (DIY)

1. **On Android:** Use built-in screen recorder (pull down notification shade → Screen Record)
2. **On iPhone:** Add Screen Recording to Control Centre → tap to start
3. **On Desktop:** Use OBS Studio (free) or Windows Game Bar (Win+G)
4. Follow the scene script above, recording each section separately
5. Edit in CapCut or DaVinci Resolve — add the voiceover text as captions
