How to Use eHisaab

💸
eHisaab is a free, offline expense-splitting app. All data stays on your device — no internet needed after install.
  1. 1
    Sign Up / Sign In
    Open the app → enter your name, email and password → tap Sign Up. Next time just Sign In with the same email & password.
  2. 2
    Import Contacts (optional)
    Tap your avatar (top right) → My Contacts → Import from Phone. This lets you auto-fill member names when creating groups.
  3. 3
    Create a Group
    Tap + New Group → choose a name, currency, and category → select members → tap Create Group. You are always added automatically.
  4. 4
    Add Expenses
    Inside the group → tap + Add Expense → fill in description, amount, and how to split. The Paid by field defaults to Me (you) — change it if someone else paid → tap Save.
  5. 5
    Check & Settle Balances
    Go to the Balances tab to see who owes what. Tap Settle next to a name once a payment is made.
💡 Tip: Install as an app — tap Add to Home Screen in your browser for a native app experience.
👥 Managing Groups
➕
Create a Group
Tap + New Group. Pick a name, currency (₹/$€£¥), and category (Trip / Party / Home / Food / Other). Your imported contacts appear with checkboxes — select who to add.
👤
Add Members Later
Open the group → Members tab → type a name in the field → optionally add their WhatsApp number → tap Add.
📱
WhatsApp Numbers
Members without a number show a +📱 button. Tap it to add their number. Once saved, tap 📲 to send them a balance reminder on WhatsApp.
📦
Archive / Delete
On the home screen, tap the archive icon on a group card to hide it. Use ☐ Select to bulk-delete multiple groups at once.
🎉
Party Groups
Choose Party as category to unlock the Bottle Calculator — enter drink types, bottle size, price, and glass counts per person for auto drink-cost splitting.
🗑️
Bulk Remove Members
In the Members tab tap ☐ Select, check names, then tap Remove Selected. Only removes from group — does not delete their expenses.
💸 Adding & Managing Expenses
  1. 1
    Add an Expense
    Inside a group → tap + Add Expense → enter description, amount, and date. The Paid by dropdown defaults to Me (your name) — select another member if they paid. Choose a category icon for quick recognition.
  2. 2
    Split Options
    Equal — divide evenly among all members.
    Exact ₹ — type the exact amount each person owes.
    % Share — enter a percentage for each person.
  3. 3
    Edit or Delete an Expense
    Tap any expense row → tap the ✏️ Edit or 🗑 Delete button in the detail view.
  4. 4
    Bulk Delete Expenses
    In the Expenses tab tap ☐ Select → check expenses → tap 🗑 Delete Selected. Useful for clearing test entries.
  5. 5
    Recurring Expenses
    Tick Mark as recurring when saving an expense. Later, in the Analytics tab tap 🔁 Re-add Recurring to re-enter all recurring expenses in one tap.
  6. 6
    Search Expenses
    Use the 🔍 search bar in the Expenses tab to filter by description keyword.
💡 Tip: If you use the Bottle Calculator for a Party group, do not add a separate drink expense manually — it will double-count.
⚖️ Balances & Settlements
📊
Member Balances Table
The Balances tab shows every member with:
• Total Paid — how much they paid for the group
• Net Balance — green = gets back, red = owes.
💳
Settlement Plan
Below the member table, the Settlement Plan shows the minimum number of payments needed to clear all debts. Tap Settle to mark a payment as done.
📲
Send WhatsApp Reminder
Tap the 📲 button next to a settlement row to send an automatic WhatsApp message to that person with the amount they owe.
✅
Mark as Settled
When someone pays, tap Settle → confirm the amount → tap Confirm Settlement. The balance updates instantly.
📈
Analytics
The Analytics tab shows a bar chart of total spending per member and a full activity log of every change made to the group.
🧮
Stats Bar & Debt Summary
The top of every group shows 4 chips: Total Spent, Expenses, Members, and Your Balance. Below them a colour-coded row shows the full settlement plan at a glance — red = you owe, green = you get back, grey = between others.
⚙️ Settings & Security
  1. 🔒
    Set a PIN Lock
    Avatar → Set PIN Lock → enter a 4–6 digit PIN. Next time you open the app, it will ask for your PIN before showing any data.
  2. 🌙
    Dark Mode
    Tap the 🌙 moon icon in the top header to switch between light and dark themes. The setting is remembered automatically.
  3. 👥
    Manage Contacts
    Avatar → My Contacts → import from your phone, or add contacts manually. These contacts auto-appear as checkboxes when creating new groups.
  4. ⬇️
    Export Backup
    Avatar → Export Backup → saves a .json file of all your groups and expenses. Keep it safe — it's your data insurance.
  5. ⬆️
    Import Backup
    Avatar → Import Backup → pick your .json file to restore data on a new device or after clearing browser data.
  6. 🔔
    Notifications
    Avatar → Enable Notifications → allow the browser permission. eHisaab will send reminders for pending settlements.
💡 Tip: Export a backup before clearing your browser cache or switching devices. Your data is stored locally and will be lost if the browser data is cleared.
